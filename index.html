Space Exploration & Astronomy

Space exploration is humanity’s quest to understand the universe beyond Earth. From robotic probes to human missions, space agencies around the world are uncovering the mysteries of planets, stars, and galaxies. A recent milestone was India’s Chandrayaan-3 mission, which successfully landed near the Moon’s south pole in 2023—making India the first country to achieve this.
